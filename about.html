About Us

Welcome to eDoc, your trusted online platform for scheduling appointments with doctors. Our mission is to make healthcare more accessible and convenient for everyone. Whether you are a patient looking to book an appointment, a doctor managing your schedule, or an administrator overseeing the system, eDoc is here to help.

Our platform allows patients to easily find and book appointments with doctors based on their specialties. Doctors can manage their schedules and view appointment requests from patients. Administrators can add, edit, and delete doctors, schedule sessions, and view patient details.

We are committed to providing a seamless and efficient experience for all our users. Thank you for choosing eDoc for your healthcare needs.
